Billerica School Names

The people behind the names on Billerica’s schools.

School names have a way of becoming familiar without being fully known. They appear on report cards, yearbooks, sports schedules, bus routes, and town maps, but the lives behind those names can fade into the background.

This series looks at the people behind several Billerica school names: educators, public figures, veterans, civic leaders, and local families whose names became part of the town’s everyday geography.

The focus is not simply on why a school was named, but on who these people were, what they did, and how their stories fit into Billerica’s larger history.
