Billerica School Names

This project explores the men whose names were given to Billerica public schools.

The goal was not to write exhaustive biographies, but to answer a simpler question: who were these people, and why were they remembered by the town through its schools?

The series was written during the spring of 2026 and focuses primarily on schools that were still active or still standing when the project began, with the notable exception of Vining Elementary, which had only very recently closed. John F. Kennedy School and Thomas Ditson School were intentionally not included.

These essays were originally published individually online and are collected here to make them easier to read, preserve, and search in one place. The research relied heavily on newspapers, town reports, census records, yearbooks, cemetery records, local histories, archival collections, and other publicly available materials.

These essays represent the fullest accounts I was able to assemble from the available public record and local sources at the time of writing.

Any references to current landmarks, buildings, businesses, roads, or school sites reflect Billerica as it appeared in 2026.
